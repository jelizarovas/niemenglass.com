Welcome to Niemen Glass Company in Kent, Washington

The Glass Specialists Located in Kent, Washington

Niemen Glass Company was started in Kent, Washington. Niemen Glass Company has been a local family-owned and operated glass business since 1946. Although the business changed ownership in 1977, the business model of “customer focused, quality workmanship, and competitive prices" has not changed.

We provide high-quality residential and commercial glass services to the residents of King County & Pierce County in the state of Washington. We hope to retain the trust of homeowners and commercial contractors throughout Kent, Renton, Des Moines, Auburn, Covington, and Federal Way.

Niemen Glass Company of Kent is dedicated to the standard…

“Customer Focused, Quality Workmanship, and Competitive Prices"

We continually strive to build and retain the trust of all our customers by putting your satisfaction as our number one priority.

Niemen Glass Company of Kent provides the most valuable residential and commercial glass and glazing services in Washington. We provide the finest quality glass and glass products from the leading manufacturers with the most up-to-date technological advancements in the industry, like: Kawneer, Hartung, Arcadia, US Aluminum, Holcam, Agalite, and Cardinal Shower Enclosures.

Our glass services include:

  • Residential Glass - Bringing more comfort, lower energy bills, and higher investment returns to your home.
  • Commercial Glass - Generating beautiful storefronts which elevates curbside appeal and creates a comfortable climate for customers while reducing energy costs.
  • Tenant Improvements - Quick & reliable services for tenants needs, improvements, and repairs for greater safety with aesthetically pleasing results.
  • Detailed Glass - Experienced specialized craftsmanship combining beauty and precision for superior results for all your glass projects.
We serve areas from Seattle to Tacoma including every city surrounding Northgate, Bellevue, Renton, Issaquah, Kent, Auburn, Covington, Federal Way, and SeaTac.
