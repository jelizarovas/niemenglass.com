About Niemen Glass Company Located in Kent, Washington

Niemen Glass Company was started in 1946. As a locally owned and operated glass shop in the Kent, WA area, Niemen Glass focused on customer satisfaction by providing quality workmanship at competitive prices. Although the ownership of Niemen Glass Company changed hands in July of 1977, the focus has not. We still focus on each and every customer to meet their glass needs in a professional manner with the best possible prices.

Niemen Glass services the glass needs of homeowners and commercial contractors in Kent, Renton, Des Moines, Auburn, Covington, and Federal Way, Washington. Our expert mobile glass service can be dispatched to your home or office to handle your every glass need quickly.

Why Choose Niemen Glass Company

Niemen Glass strictly focuses on glass and glazing.  We carry a full line of glass and glass products from the leading manufacturers in the industry such as Kawneer, Arcadia, US Aluminum, Cardinal Shower Enclosures, and many others. Niemen Glass Company not only uses the best quality glass products on the market, but our glass experts can install or repair your glass for any residential or commercial application. We are confident in the products and service we provide and hope you put your trust in Niemen Glass Company to meet all your glass needs.

We serve areas from Seattle to Tacoma including every city surrounding Northgate, Bellevue, Renton, Issaquah, Kent, Auburn, Covington, Federal Way, and SeaTac.
