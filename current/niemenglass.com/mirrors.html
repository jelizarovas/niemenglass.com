Custom Fabricated Mirrors

Because mirrors are the perfect accent to reflect both your decor and style, you want to make sure you choose the right mirror for your settings. A beautiful mirror can lend character and charm to your bedroom or foyer; it can also add a personal touch to a basic bathroom. Mirrors can provide an additional light source and add a dimension of depth and beauty to any room in your home. Mirroring your walls is an excellent and affordable way to increase the "size" and appearance of any room, without adding the high cost of remodeling.

The mirror specialist at Niemen Glass Company can craft a mirror to match your exact specifications. From seamed & polished edges to beveled edges, we do it all. No job is too large or small. Allow the mirror experts at Niemen Glass Company to handle the addition of mirrors to your home from fabrication to professional installation.
