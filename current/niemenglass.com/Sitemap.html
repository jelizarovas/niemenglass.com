Why Choose Niemen Glass?

Niemen Glass strictly focuses on glass and glazing. We are a proud member of Master Builders, and we carry a full line of glass and glass products from the leading manufacturers in the industry like Kawneer, Arcadia, US Aluminum, Cardinal Shower Enclosures, and many others. Niemen Glass Company not only uses the best quality glass products on the market, but our glass experts can install or repair your glass for any residential or commercial application. We know who we are and hope you put your trust in Niemen Glass Company to meet all your glass needs.
