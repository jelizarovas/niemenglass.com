Tenant Improvements for Commercial Properties

One of the Niemen Glass Company specialties is Tenant Improvement or “TI.” Whether you are replacing existing windows to make your building more energy efficient or you want to create a more appealing and inviting look, Niemen Glass has over 30 years of experience working with contractors, architects, designers, and owners in the Tenant Improvement construction field. We know how important it is to keep your project on time and within budget for that final look your team wants. Everything from heavy glass entries, new glass partition walls, mirrors, and even windows can be installed with a variety of color, pattern, and texture options for a more customize look. Niemen Glass has the experience to get it done using quality products and expert installers.
