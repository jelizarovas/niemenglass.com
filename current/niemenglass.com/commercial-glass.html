Commercial Glass in Kent, Washington

When glass breaks, it becomes both a safety concern and a security risk for your business. Your employees or customers could be in danger of serious injury and your business may no longer be secure. In such circumstances, the amount of time it takes to replace or repair the broken glass is of the utmost importance. Niemen Glass Company can be on site quickly to fix any commercial glass problem to make your business safe and secure again.

If you need glass repaired or replaced in your business, call your local, full service, commercial glass provider. Niemen Glass Company has the experience and expertise to answer all your questions and resolve all your commercial glass repair and commercial glass replacement issues. Niemen will provide you with the facts needed to make an informed decision.

Niemen Glass Company is licensed and insured and is committed to complete customer satisfaction by offering top-quality products and professional service.
