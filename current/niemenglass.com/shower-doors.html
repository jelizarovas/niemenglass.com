Frameless Glass Shower Doors & Glass Tub Enclosures

If you are considering a bathroom remodeling project, consider a frameless glass shower enclosure. Frameless glass shower doors are a great investment and will dramatically increase the resale value of your home. Glass shower enclosures are easy to clean and maintain. They add a unique, luxurious feel to any bathroom and are engineered to last a lifetime. The frameless shower door experts at Niemen Glass Company can custom fabricate and install new glass shower doors to any size bathroom, large or small. Niemen Glass will walk you through the entire process from estimating, pricing, installation, and follow-up support for your new frameless glass shower enclosure.

There are many options of frameless glass shower doors available on the market today, like using patterned glass for additional style. Our shower door technicians can walk you through selecting the glass style, color, pattern, and thickness perfect for your overall bathroom design. Glass shower doors will give your bath an elegant look and if you live in or near Kent, Washington, you can’t go wrong by choosing Niemen Glass Company.

Glass Shower Enclosures

Glass shower enclosures basically come in two different types: framed and frameless. Frameless (no metal framing) is the more contemporary option, but some homeowners prefer the more traditional look of using a metal frame to hold the shower enclosure glass in place. Niemen Glass will cater to your preference. Just like the variety of glass styles, there are many metal framing options from which to choose. Our goal at Niemen Glass is to make sure you are completely satisfied with the entire process and finished product. Give us a call for a free in-home estimate on your bathroom remodel.
